Morning Coffee

The Delicious Cup

Is there anything more satisfying than that first sip of coffee inthe morning? Whether you're a latte drink or you take yours black, there's no questions that coffee is energizing and unbelievably delicious. Plus, that smell. It's unreal.

We could go on and on about how great coffee tastes, but let's be real. You already know that. So in honor of everyone's favorite hot beverage, here are a few things you probably didn't know about your morning cup of coffee.

Did you know?:

  • We drink over 500 billion cups of coffee every year worldwide.
  • If you're a woman who drinks coffee, you maybe at lower risk for depression.
  • Coffee lowers your risk of Alzheimer's disease.
